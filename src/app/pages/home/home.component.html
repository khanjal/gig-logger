Description

Gig Logger is a tool to input gig work into your own google spreadsheet to track information.

Features

  • Quickly store trips in groups
  • Data stored is easily accessible

Pages

home - This page

add - Add trip

settings - Settings

Instructions

  1. Create a new Google spreadsheet
  2. Open File > Share > Share with others
  3. Add srvsheets@gig-sheets.iam.gserviceaccount.com as an editor
  4. Do not share your Google sheeet ID with anyone!

FAQ

  • Do I need to create an account?

    You only need an account for Google sheets. No account is needed for this website.

  • Is it free?

    Yes. There is no reason to monetize this very useful site.

  • How often should I save?

    Save as often as you want! You can save after each trip, shift, or just once at the end of the day.

  • How do I edit saved data?

    Once data is saved to the spreadsheet you'll need to go to Google sheets on your phone or computer to change data.

  • Why does it take a while to save?

    A lot of calculations running will slow down the saving as it waits for the calculations to run.

  • Is my data safe?

    Your data is stored in your browser and Google sheets. Gig logger doesn't store any of your data.

  • Can I sync accross multiple computers?

    You can view your sheet data on multiple browsers but your local data is only available in that browser.

  • How do I delete my data?

    On the setup page you can click delete all data to remove your local and remote data. Your spreadsheet data will remain untouched.
