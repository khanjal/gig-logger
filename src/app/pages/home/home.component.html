Common Questions

expand_more

Is my data safe?

Your data stays in your Google Sheets. We don't store anything on our servers.

Does it work offline?

Yes! Log trips offline and they'll sync when you're back online.

Can I use multiple devices?

Yes, your Google Sheet syncs across devices. However, avoid using the app simultaneously on multiple devices with the same sheet to prevent data conflicts. Use one device at a time during your shift.

How often should I save?

Enable auto-save and forget about it. Manual saves work after each trip or shift too.

What about taxes?

Track all your miles and earnings to help organize information you may need for financial or tax reporting. For specific tax advice, consult a professional.
